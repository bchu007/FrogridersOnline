Instructions

You and your friend are frogridders. In this game of leapfrog, you guys take turns choosing a frog to ride in order to jump over an adjacent frog into any empty lilypad. You collect the frog you jump over.

Some frogs are more valuable than others. Brown, blue, yellow, red is the order of most to least valuable. Plan your routes to jump over consequtive frogs.

Now go round up those croakers!
